About Me

Kingsley is a third year Software Engineering student at Queen's University with a specialization in programming and innovation. Originating from Toronto Ontario, this talented individual stepped into programming in with Flash and Visual Basic, which sparked his interest for greater challenges. By exploring various programming opportunities through courses, projects, internships, and co-op placements, Kingsley is familliar with agile software development principles, startup culture, a myriad of programming languages, business management, and entrepreneurship.

In his most recent experience as an agile software engineer specializing in iOS development, Kingsley has implemented OOP methodologies, UI Design, and MVC pattern at a FinTech startup. Previous experiences also include civil engineering design, 3d modeling/printing, and energy conservation resulting in strong proficiency with technology and programming. In addition, Kingsley also specializes in web development and database management.

His passions lie with all elements of music, such as producing, writing, composing, and performing. Initially raised as a classical pianist from a young age, his love for music has sparked a creativity spanning multiple genres and instruments. As an adventurous fidler, Kingsley blends electronic elements to create unique atmospheres, aesthetics, and sounds.
